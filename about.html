The App

How ScrollOff Works

ScrollOff helps you earn your screen time. Instead of mindlessly opening apps, you earn time credits by staying focused, then spend those credits when you actually want to browse. It turns screen time into a conscious choice, not a reflex.

🎯

Choose Apps to Block

Pick the apps that distract you most: social media, games, streaming, whatever your weakness is.

⏳

Earn Credits

5 minutes earned per hour of staying focused. Your discipline becomes currency.

✨

Spend Guilt-Free

Spend your earned credits for a timed peek. No guilt. No shame. You earned it.

📊

Track Progress

See your patterns improve over time with 7-day or 30-day usage insights.

What We Believe

Principles We Build On

🎯

Rewards Over Punishment

Earning screen time feels different from having it taken away. Positive reinforcement creates lasting habits.

🔒

Privacy Is Non-Negotiable

100% on-device processing. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. We literally cannot see your data.

🤝

Self-Accountability

Not parental control. ScrollOff is for adults who want to manage their own screen time on their own terms.

🧠

Science-Backed Design

Implementation intentions, positive reinforcement, habit loops. Every feature is rooted in behavioral psychology.

Built With

Technology Stack

Built natively with SwiftUI and Apple's Screen Time API, ScrollOff processes everything locally on your device. No servers. No accounts. No analytics. No tracking.

SwiftUI Swift 5.9+ SwiftData Screen Time API StoreKit 2 Combine

Android coming soon, built with Jetpack Compose, Kotlin Multiplatform, and Material 3.
