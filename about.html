Built Because We Were Tired Too

We believe that technology should enhance our lives, not consume them. ScrollOff was created to help people develop healthier relationships with their devices and reclaim time for what truly matters.

Our Mission

Technology Should Enhance Your Life

ScrollOff is an iOS app designed to help you break free from endless scrolling and reclaim your most valuable resource: time. Instead of punishing you with hard limits and shame notifications, we built a reward-based system where you earn guilt-free scroll time by staying focused.

Every screen time app follows the same broken playbook: set a limit, hit the limit, feel guilty. Tap "Ignore Limit" because you need to reply to that message. Feel even more guilty. Repeat until you delete the app entirely.

The problem was never you. The problem was the approach.

Who We Are

Sugrowth — Building Tools for Personal Growth

Sugrowth is building tools for personal growth and productivity. ScrollOff is our flagship product, born from the belief that positive reinforcement creates lasting habits where punishment-based approaches consistently fail.

Built natively with SwiftUI and Apple's Screen Time API, ScrollOff processes everything locally on your device. No servers. No accounts. No analytics. No tracking. We literally cannot see your data, even if we wanted to.

ScrollOff is designed for individual self-management — not parental control. Users voluntarily choose which apps to block on their own device, and earn credits by staying focused that they can spend on guilt-free peek sessions whenever they choose.
