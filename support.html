Frequently Asked Questions

Download ScrollOff from the App Store, open it, and grant Screen Time permission when prompted. Then choose which apps you want to block, set your daily schedule, and you're ready. The app will guide you through each step.

ScrollOff uses Apple's Screen Time API to monitor which apps you use and to block them when needed. This is the same secure system that Apple's built-in Screen Time uses. All processing happens locally on your device — no data is sent anywhere.

You earn credits automatically by staying off your blocked apps. The longer you stay focused, the more credits you accumulate. Premium and Pro users can also earn bonus credits through Focus Challenges. Credits can then be spent on "peek" sessions to use your blocked apps guilt-free.

During a No-Go Zone, all blocked apps are completely inaccessible — no peeks, no workarounds, no exceptions (unless you use an Emergency Token). This is designed for times when you need absolute protection, like sleep hours or deep work sessions.

Subscriptions are managed through Apple's App Store. To request a refund, go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone, or visit reportaproblem.apple.com. Apple handles all subscription billing directly.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Apps aren't being blocked

Make sure Screen Time permission is granted. Go to Settings > Screen Time and ensure ScrollOff has access. If the issue persists, try restarting your device and reopening ScrollOff.

Credits aren't accumulating

Credits only accumulate when you have active blocked apps set up and you're staying off them during your configured schedule. Check that your blocking schedule is active and that you have at least one app blocked.

Biometric Lock isn't working

Ensure Face ID or Touch ID is set up on your device in Settings > Face ID & Passcode. Then enable Biometric Lock within ScrollOff's settings. If it still doesn't work, try toggling the feature off and on again.

Subscription not recognized

Try restoring purchases within the app. If that doesn't work, make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the purchase. You can also check your subscription status in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.
