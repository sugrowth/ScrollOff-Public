## üéØ MAJOR NEW FEATURES

### 1. Quiet Hours
**What it is**: Scheduled blocking with NO penalties or wallet loss
**Key Features**:
- Set up time windows when selected apps are automatically blocked
- Create up to 10 different modes (Work, Sleep, Custom, etc.)
- Default Work mode: 9 AM - 5 PM (Mon-Fri)
- Default Sleep mode: 10 PM - 7 AM
- No wallet deduction, no penalties - just a gentle pause
- App limits per tier: Free (3 apps), Premium/Pro (unlimited)
- Minimum duration: 5 minutes
- Maximum duration: 23 hours 59 minutes

**Screenshots**: IMG_6429.PNG

### 2. Milestones & Progress Tracking
**What it is**: Track your self-control achievements and challenges
**Key Features**:
- **Milestones tracked**:
  - Forfeited sessions (with time used)
  - Emergency tokens purchased and used
  - Hours protected in No-Go-Zone
  - Completed vs abandoned sessions

- **Daily cap**: 3 milestone ledger entries per day
- **Minimum interval**: 6 hours between milestone recordings
- **Progress Spotlight**: 7-day summary showing time earned vs spent
- **Motivational messages**: Contextual encouragement based on progress

**Screenshots**: IMG_6425.PNG, IMG_6430.PNG

### 3. Focus Challenge
**What it is**: Timed challenges to earn bonus game tokens
**Key Features**:
- Set challenge duration (1-12 hours)
- Must start between 6 AM and midnight
- Earn 30 bonus seconds per hour of successful focus
- Track completed vs abandoned challenges
- Real-time countdown timer
- Stats: Played, Won, Lost tracking
